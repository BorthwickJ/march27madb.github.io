Google Knows Everything About You!

Google collects ALL of your data such as name, age, gender, location, email address, and phone number.

Is Your Data Safe and is it Yours?

Google have had numerous data leaks throughout their history. How safe is your data, and is it really yours?

Is it possible to escape Google?

From search to maps, emails to smart devices—Google is everywhere. How much of your life is really private?

What Can You Do?

  • Use alternative search engines like DuckDuckGo or Startpage.
  • Turn off location tracking in your Google account settings.
  • Regularly review and delete your Google data.
  • Use privacy-focused browsers like Brave or Firefox.
  • Consider self-hosted email solutions instead of Gmail.
