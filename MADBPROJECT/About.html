Rise of Google

A brief look at the history of Google from their creation in 1998 to their expansion, looking at the Google product range such as Maps, Android and Gmail.

Privacy Issues

Exploring Google and the numerous data leaks such as street view in 2010 and Google+ in 2018. Also, a discussion of the reporcussions and fines that Google had to pay.

Innovation Supression

Investigating how Google have negatively impacted innovation in the search engine industry, and how they have become a powerful and dangerous monopoly.

Aim of This Website

The aim of this website is to explore the rise of Google from a simple search engine to a dominant force in the online world. By looking at Privacy issues and the Suppression of innovation this website will highlight why Google need to be stopped.
